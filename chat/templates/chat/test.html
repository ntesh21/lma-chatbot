LAMA ENERGY GROUP acquires DIGI CZ from RCS&RDS

LAMA ENERGY GROUP s.r.o. (LAMA), a Czech privately owned investment group engaged primarily in the exploration, production and sale of oil & gas and production and sale of utilities, has acquired DIGI Czech Republic s.r.o. (DIGI CZ) from RCS & RDS S.A. (RCS&RDS), a major telecommunication operator in South-Eastern Europe head-quartered in Romania. DIGI CZ is a leading Czech direct-to-home (DTH) operator. The transaction was completed on 21th April, 2015. The parties have agreed not to disclose the transaction value.
